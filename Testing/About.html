Who We Are

About Us

We began with the desire to provide quality promotional items and gifts manufactured in China with outstanding service. Our desire was to make your life easier and make the process of buying promotional items pain free. That was almost a decade ago, but even today this value is in everything we do. We like to think that we are people-centric.

We are currently exporting to 35 markets worldwide and working with some of the leading suppliers in the promotional items industry.

Our Philosophy

At HK Genuine, we are passionate about friendly, client-oriented service and we love that we can provide this with high-quality promotional products made in China. We believe honesty, transparency, and good communication are at the heart of every good business relationship and are central to every interaction. We understand that quality and on-time shipment are what you need in order to make your business run smoothly. To that end, we maintain an in-house QC team to monitor production and inspect your order before shipment. We also know that getting quotes can be less than pain-free. So to improve the experience, we guarantee quotes in an hour or less or your samples are free.

Custom Orders

If you see something that interests you, please take a few minutes and contact us. If you don't see what you're looking for, we also do custom product orders - please send us some information and one of our sales team will get in touch with you. We look forward to working with you.
