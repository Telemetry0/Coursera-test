Who We Are

About Us

Hong Kong Genuine began almost a decade ago with a single employee. Through hard work and determination, HK Genuine has grown to 15 full-time employees with an annual revenue of over $6M USD and strong projected growth. We are headquartered in the Panyu district of Guangzhou and exporting to 35 markets worldwide with an established history with some of the leaders in the promotional items industry.

Our Philosophy

At HK Genuine, we are focused on friendly, customer-oriented service selling trendy, high quality promotional products made in China. We specialize in silicon products, but have extensive experience with most common materials including plastics, PU, leather, and various fabrics. We have an in-house QC team to ensure that high quality is maintained throughout production and shipment is on time. We take great pride in giving you the level of customer service that you deserve and have come to expect. Moreover, we believe honesty, transparency, and good communication are at the heart of every good business relationship and are central to every interaction.
